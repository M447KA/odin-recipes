Description

A creamy yogurt soup made with wheat grains, herbs and sometimes chickpeas, served warm or cold.

Ingredients

  • Yogurt (plain, unsweetened)
  • Water or broth
  • Wheat grains (Dzavar or pearl barley)
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, dill, cilantro)
  • Egg (optional, for thickening)
  • Chickpeas (cooked)
  • Garlic (minced)

Steps

  1. Cook Grains: Boil wheat grains until tender.
  2. Prepare Yogurt Base: Whisk yogurt with water or broth; heat gently, stirring constantly.
  3. Combine: Add cooked grains and seasonings; simmer briefly.
  4. Add Herbs: Stir in fresh herbs before serving.
  5. Serve: Enjoy warm or chilled.
