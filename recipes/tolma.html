Tolma

Description

A traditional Armenian dish of grape leaves or cabbage rolls stuffed with a savory mixture of ground meat, rice, and herbs often served with garlic yogurt.

Ingredients

  • Ground meat (beef, lamb, or a mix)
  • Rice
  • Onion (finely chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, dill, cilantro)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika (optional)
  • Tomato paste (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare Leaves: Rinse grape leaves or blanch cabbage leaves until soft.
  2. Mix Filling: Combine ground meat, rice, onion, garlic, herbs, salt, and spices.
  3. Wrap Tolma: Place filling on a leaf, fold sides, and roll tightly.
  4. Cook: Arrange rolls in a pot, add water or broth to cover, and simmer until tender.
  5. Serve: Enjoy with garlic yogurt.
