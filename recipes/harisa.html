Harisa

Description

A hearty porridge made from slow-cooked wheat and shredded meat, symbolizing resilience and tradition.

Ingredients

  • Hulled wheat (or pearled barley)
  • Chicken or lamb (bone-in for flavor)
  • Water or broth
  • Fresh herbs (parsley or cilantro)
  • Ground paprika

Steps

  1. Cook Meat: Boil chicken or lamb in water until tender, then shred the meat and set aside.
  2. Cook Wheat: Add hulled wheat to the cooking broth; simmer until soft and thickened.
  3. Combine: Mix shredded meat back into the pot, stirring to create a smooth, porridge-like consistency.
  4. Season: Add salt, pepper, and optional butter.
  5. Serve: Garnish as desired and enjoy warm.
